About ADC

The nation's largest Arab American grassroots organization

Founded in 1980 by former Senator James Abourezk, the American-Arab Anti-Discrimination Committee (ADC) is the largest Arab American grassroots organization in the United States, representing approximately 3.7 million Arab Americans. ADC is committed to defending and promoting the human rights, civil rights, and liberties of all people of Arab heritage.

Civil Rights Protection

Safeguarding the rights and freedoms of Arab Americans and people of Arab heritage through legal advocacy, counseling, and litigation support.

Combat Discrimination

Fighting stereotypes, prejudice, and hate crimes targeting Arab Americans while providing legal assistance through our civil rights hotline.

Community Advocacy

Serving as the public voice for Arab Americans on domestic and international policy matters that affect our community.

Cultural Education

Enhancing public understanding of Arab history, heritage, and contributions to American society through outreach and programming.

Social Justice

Partnering with marginalized communities domestically and internationally to advance social justice for all people.

Legal Services

Providing legal assistance through our national hotline at 844-ADC-9955 for cases of discrimination, defamation, and hate crimes.

ADC Tampa Chapter

Serving the Tampa Bay Arab American community

Our Local Mission

ADC Tampa is proud to be one of ADC's growing network of local chapters, bringing the national organization's mission directly to the Tampa Bay community. We work to protect the civil rights of Arab Americans in our region while fostering understanding and appreciation of Arab culture.

What We Do

Our chapter serves as a local resource for Arab Americans in the Tampa Bay area, connecting community members with national ADC resources while organizing local initiatives.

  • Local advocacy and civil rights support
  • Community events and cultural programming
  • Educational outreach and awareness campaigns
  • Connection to national ADC legal resources
  • Voter registration and civic engagement
  • Building coalitions with allied organizations

Join Our Community

Whether you're Arab American or simply committed to civil rights and cultural understanding, ADC Tampa welcomes you. Together, we can build a stronger, more inclusive Tampa Bay.
